The Callisto Protocol: prequel podcast Helix Station released

The first episodes are already here

After a long wait, The Callisto Protocol will be released on December 2nd and the excitement should have increased immeasurably for some. If you want to bridge the waiting time, you now have the opportunity, because apart from the trailers and game impressions, the history of The Callisto Protocol is now available as a podcast.

The Callisto Protocol: Helix Station podcast released

The podcast provides a prequel to The Callisto Protocol and is called The Callisto Protocol: Helix Station . Accordingly, it is a prequel. This consists of a total of six episodes, each lasting about 25 minutes. It is a so-called scripted podcast, i.e. a fictional story similar to an audio book.

The first two episodes of The Callisto Protocol: Helix Station were released on November 3rd at 6 a.m. local time. It continues with Episode 3 on November 10th, also since 6 a.m. The full release schedule for The Callisto Protocol: Helix Station podcast is as follows:

  • Episode 1: November 3 - 06:00
  • Episode 2: November 3 - 06:00
  • Episode 3: November 10 – 06:00
  • Episode 4: November 17 – 06:00
  • Episode 5: November 24 – 06:00
  • Episode 6: December 1 - 06:00

The Callisto Protocol: Helix Station podcast episodes can be accessed on major podcast platforms including Stitcher, Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Google Podcasts, Sirius, Pandora, iHeartRadio, Pocket Casts and TuneIn.
