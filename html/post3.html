PS5 Exclusive: Silent Hill 2 Remake Officially Confirmed

The remake of the horror classic is in the hands of Bloober Team.

The rumor mill has been churning for months, with the community speculating about what is or isn't happening in the Silent Hill universe - and now it's official. Yes, a remake of Silent Hill 2 is in the works. Konami announced this during the Silent Hill Transmission live stream.

Silent Hill 2 Remake for PS5

The audience was informed via the live stream and via Twitter that Silent Hill 2 is undergoing a complete overhaul to once again strike terror into the gaming community. Silent Hill 2 is a console exclusive for PlayStation 5 only, but can also be purchased for PC via STEAM. As part of the announcement, Konami also released a trailer for the Silent Hill 2 remake and a few more details.

This is how the Silent Hill 2 remake lets you experience the foggy city in 4K resolution. Bloober Team is responsible for the production of the game, which relies on the use of the Unreal Engine. Masahiro Ito could be won for the creature design, while Akira Yamaoka takes care of the background music. The latter spoke on the live stream about the challenges of creating the music for the Silent Hill 2 remake and why he wanted to create a new kind of music.

"The remake also features a new music style, new challenges, and new sound design and [new] music that will delight existing fans."

And what can you expect from the game itself? Bloober Team didn't quite let the cat out of the bag, but emphasized that Mateusz said their goal was to inherit everything that made the original a timeless classic while delving deeper into the misty streets of Silent Hill Lenart, director of the new edition.

Considering that for years Silent Hill 2 has been considered one of the universes that defined the horror genre in the video game sector, the decision to launch a remake is entirely understandable. That you want to modernize the game, but at the same time stay true to the original, also.
